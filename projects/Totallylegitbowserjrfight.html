TotallyLegitBowserJrFight

Mario and Luigi have to defeat Bowser Jr. But maybe the creators of the world decided to make things a bit strange.

It's up to you to find out what funny sequence of events leads you to your next adventure...

Github Bossfight Page

Reflection: It was fun working together and creating funny scenarios for results of either attacking or guarding. Adding more pictures and gifs would have made this project more aesthetically pleasing, however it would have taken even more files than the 20+ we had in the IDE already. Some things we struggled on during this project was the planning, it start off extreemely messy and confusing, even though we had made many labels and color codes to organize the events by. I guess it is the consequences of having so many files, but eventually we both came to an agreement to some specific ways to organize the files. Aron also had a really good idea for marking files that worked/ are bugged with markers so we would know which ones to go back to fizx and which ones to confirm were perfect.
